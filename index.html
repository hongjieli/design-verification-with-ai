Design Verification with AI

A structured portfolio of AI-assisted design verification topics, organized around eight practical project directions: CoverPilot, RegMatrix, TestForge RL, DVCore, WaveScope MCP, FailSense, JiraMine, and RegCleanse.

View GitHub Repo Open Documentation
Overview

AI fits DV best as a copilot for real engineering artifacts

Verification teams work across logs, coverage, specs, assertions, tests, waveforms, and bug history. AI becomes most valuable when it helps summarize, connect, prioritize, and explain these artifacts instead of pretending to replace signoff judgment.

Topic 1

CoverPilot

Coverage intelligence for hole analysis, RTL mapping, closure guidance, and next-step recommendations.

Topic 2

RegMatrix

Register/spec intelligence for document parsing, dependency extraction, dynamic constraints, and test suggestion.

Topic 3

TestForge RL

Budget-aware regression optimization using RL or adaptive strategies for higher-value test selection.

Topic 4

DVCore

The agent foundation for AI-native DV workflows, including orchestration, memory, and subagent routing.

Topic 5

WaveScope MCP

Waveform-aware debug intelligence focused on event extraction, suspicious signals, and root-cause acceleration.

Topic 6

FailSense

Regression failure intelligence for clustering, novelty detection, ownership hints, and triage summaries.

Topic 7

JiraMine

Historical issue intelligence that mines JIRA and bug history to identify test cases most likely to expose real RTL bugs.

Topic 8

RegCleanse

Regression-history intelligence that finds consistently low-yield test cases and supports retirement, demotion, or lighter execution policies.
